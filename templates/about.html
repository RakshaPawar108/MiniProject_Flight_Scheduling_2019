About FirstClass


"The good thing about flying is that, it's never boring."

Nurtured from the love for flying, FirstClass intends to offer the best flights for all who love flying around the world.


FirstClass is the brainchild of Raksha Pawar, in the year 2019 as her Engineering Mini Project in the Third year. A lover of flying herself, she wishes to offer the best services to all who wish to travel around the world.


Just like it's name, FirstClass offers services that are only found in Business class of planes or to VIP customers because FirstClass believes that every person deserves to have a first class treatment and a happy journey full of amazing memories.


In the end, it's all about travelling...
